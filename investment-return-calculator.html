What is CAGR?

CAGR (Compound Annual Growth Rate) measures the annual growth rate of an investment over time, assuming profits are reinvested. It's the best way to compare different investments.

CAGR Formula

CAGR = (Final Value / Initial Value)^(1/Years) - 1

Why CAGR Matters

  • Compare investments: Different time periods become comparable
  • Smooth volatility: Shows average annual return, ignoring ups and downs
  • Project future: Estimate long-term growth potential
