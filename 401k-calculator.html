Understanding 401k

A 401k is an employer-sponsored retirement account. You contribute pre-tax dollars, your employer may match, and your money grows tax-deferred until retirement.

2024 401k Limits

  • Employee contribution: $23,000
  • Catch-up (50+): Additional $7,500
  • Total limit (with employer): $69,000

Pro Tip: Always contribute at least enough to get the full employer match. It's free money!
