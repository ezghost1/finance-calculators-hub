# Finance Calculators Hub - Humans.txt

## Site
Finance Calculators Hub is a free, privacy-focused platform providing 150+ online calculators for finance, health, math, and everyday life.

## Mission
To make financial tools accessible to everyone, without ads, without signup, completely free.

## Technology

- Vanilla JavaScript
