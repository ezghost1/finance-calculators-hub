How to Use This Calculator

  1. Enter the home price you're planning to buy
  2. Input your down payment (typical: 3-20%)
  3. Enter the interest rate (current average: ~6.5% for 30-year fixed)
  4. Select your loan term (15, 20, or 30 years)
  5. Click Calculate to see your monthly payment

Understanding Your Mortgage

Your monthly mortgage payment includes principal + interest, and may also include property taxes and insurance (PITI).
