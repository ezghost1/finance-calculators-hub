Understanding Interest Rates

Interest rates determine how much you pay to borrow money or earn on savings. Even small rate differences can mean thousands over time.

APR vs APY

  • APR (Annual Percentage Rate): The yearly interest rate without compounding
  • APY (Annual Percentage Yield): The effective rate including compounding
