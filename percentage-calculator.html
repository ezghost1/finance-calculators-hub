How to Calculate Percentages

Percentages are used everywhere - discounts, tips, taxes, and more. Here's how to calculate them:

  • Percentage of a number: (Percentage ÷ 100) × Number
  • What percent: (Part ÷ Whole) × 100
  • Percentage change: ((New - Old) ÷ Old) × 100
